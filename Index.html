About This Page

This web page is a demonstration of semantic HTML5 structure, accessibility features, and beginner SEO techniques. It is designed to be user-friendly and easily understood by screen readers and search engines.

Why Accessibility and SEO Matter

Accessibility

Accessibility ensures that your website can be used by people of all abilities, including those using screen readers and other assistive technologies. It improves usability and expands your audience.

Search Engine Optimization

SEO helps your content get discovered by search engines. Using proper HTML structure, heading hierarchy, and meta tags can significantly improve your page's visibility.
